ABOUT ME

Your Coach for Flips, Technique & Confidence

I Teach progressive tricking — from basics to advanced, with a focus on safe technique, strength, and clean form.

Safety-first Power drills Air control

I’m a Gymnastic & tricking teacher - building strong athletes with real fundamentals: jump mechanics, takeoff timing, core control, and safe landing habits. Every class is structured like a skill tree — you unlock clean form first, then speed, then power.

Coaching philosophy

  • Progressions that protect your body and build confidence.
  • Technique-first flips — not lucky flips.
  • Strength + mobility + air-awareness = consistent results.
